Getting to SLUG Meetings

SLUG meetings are currently held at the Google Australia offices. The address of the building is: 

 Google Australia
 Level 5, Workplace 6
 48 Pirrama Road
 Pyrmont, NSW

The Google office is the big black building marked "Accenture" opposite Star City's main steps. See the picture below,

By Train

If using the trains, you can go either get off at;

  • Town Hall station, head towards Darling Harbour, walk across the Pyrmont footbridge and then follow Pirrama Road towards Star city.
  • Central station, then follow the light rail instructions.

By Bus

If using the buses, the route 443, route 448, route 690 all stop right out front of the building.

By Light Rail

If using the light rail, get off at Star City station and walk across the street.

By Car

If you drive, then you can look for parking on the suburban streets around the office, and then walk from there.
